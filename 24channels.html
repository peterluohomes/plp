1. Digital Infrastructure & Portals
  • Unique Property Website: A dedicated, branded platform showcasing unique features and capturing leads.
  • Global Real Estate Portals: Listings on major sites including Realtor.ca, Zillow, Remax.ca, and Global.Remax.com.
  • IDX Brokerage Feed: Exposure to thousands of local brokerage websites through an Internet Data Exchange feed.
  • Toronto MLS/TREB Access: Marketing to all 70,000 members via the TorontoMLS network.
  • Brokerbay Network: Pre-listing marketing to a network of 22,000 realtors before the formal listing.
  • Local SEO Content: Hyper-local blog and guide content optimized to capture organic neighborhood traffic.
2. Targeted Social & Search
  • Facebook & Instagram Marketing: Organic posts, live virtual tours, and targeted Pay-Per-Click ads.
  • Twitter & WeChat Engagement: Utilizing global platforms for updates and reaching specific demographics.
  • Google Ads (PPC/SEM): Targeted Search, Display, and Retargeting campaigns for high-intent prospects.
  • Short-Form Video & Reels: Utilizing Instagram and YouTube Reels to highlight property lifestyle.
  • QR Code Integration: Linking all physical signs and flyers directly to the property's digital ecosystem.
3. Outreach & Professional Nurturing
  • Email Marketing Campaigns: Personalized newsletters to leads, past clients, and the Sphere of Influence.
  • SMS & Mobile Advertising: Personal communication using mobile techniques and recorded messaging.
  • Realtor Network & Referrals: Presenting at realtor meetings and utilizing brokerage referral services.
  • Sphere of Influence (SOI) Nurturing: Consistent relationship management to generate word-of-mouth leads.
4. Traditional & Local Channels
  • "For Sale" Signage: Branded physical presence acting as a primary channel for property inquiries.
  • Feature Sheets & Brochures: Professionally designed materials for showings and open house events.
  • "Just Listed" Direct Mail: Cards and flyers targeting residents in the immediate area to maximize local exposure.
  • Door Hangers & Knocking: Direct neighborhood marketing and personal presentations to residents.
  • Newspaper & Magazine Marketing: Targeted outreach in community publications for a longer shelf life.
  • Open Houses & Community Events: Hosting gatherings and sponsoring events to generate local visibility.
