32-Point Marketing Plan

- Proactive Marketing Strategy -

Our 32-point plan focuses on high-impact marketing activities designed to generate massive exposure, attract global buyers, and create a competitive selling environment.

1. Global Digital Distribution

Leveraging high-traffic web portals and international networks like Realmaster.cn and RE/MAX Global to ensure your home is visible to buyers around the world.

2. Social & Multimedia Marketing

Strategic ad placement across major social platforms and the creation of immersive digital assets, including virtual tours and dedicated property sites.

3. Direct Prospecting & Referral Networks

Active daily outreach to personal databases, past clients, and centers of influence to uncover potential buyers through direct engagement.

4. Community Outreach

Comprehensive agent-to-agent networking and localized physical marketing, including high-visibility signage and targeted neighborhood mailers.

1. Global Digital Distribution
  • Submit property details and photos to the Multiple Listing Service (MLS).
  • Upload photos and virtual tours to hundreds of internet websites.
  • Create property listings on Realtor.ca with detailed features.
  • Create property listings on Remax.ca for national exposure.
  • Feature on Remax.com for broad North American reach.
  • Submit to RE/MAX Global website for international buyer access.
  • Promote on Realmaster.cn to reach the Chinese buyer market.
  • Coordinate sync of showing instructions and tour info on all global portals.
2. Social & Multimedia Marketing
  • Implement marketing campaign on Facebook.
  • Execute promotional campaign on Twitter.
  • Market property highlights on LinkedIn.
  • Engage international networks via WeChat.
  • Launch unique website at yourpropertyaddress.com.
  • Include virtual tour links in all digital descriptions.
  • Distribute professional property photos across all web platforms.
  • Utilize high-quality visual assets for all digital marketing ads.
3. Direct Prospecting & Referrals
  • Prospect and talk to 50 people per day looking for potential buyers.
  • Contact buyer leads for referrals and prospective buyers.
  • Engage "Center of Influence" for new buyer referrals.
  • Reach out to Past Clients to identify prospective buyers.
  • Distribute email marketing to personal contact lists.
  • Send "Just Listed" alerts through digital newsletters.
  • Follow up with all inquiries about your property in a timely manner.
  • Respond to feedback from salespeople who have shown the home.
4. Community Outreach
  • Email marketing campaign specifically to professional REALTORS.
  • Promote home at company sales meetings for internal matching.
  • Email feature sheets to area agents for their specific buyers.
  • Arrange for cooperating Brokers in the area to tour the home.
  • Install "For Sale" yard sign with property-specific riders.
  • Distribute "Just Listed" postcards to homes in your area.
  • Conduct Open Houses to attract active neighborhood buyers.
  • Distribute custom, full-color property brochures to all visitors.
